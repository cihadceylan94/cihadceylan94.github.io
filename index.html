Ana Sayfa Makalelerim Hobilerim Hakkımda

Wash hands with soap and water after touching animals and animal products.

When coughing and sneezing cover mouth and nose with flexed elbow or tissue.

Avoid travelling if you have a fever or cough.

If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical care early.

If you are coughing or sneezing, wear musk and must know how to use it and dispose it properly.

Eat only well cooked food.

Avoid close contact with animals that are sick.
